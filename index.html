Who We Are

Nigeria's First Car Rental Marketplace

DriveDir was founded to solve a problem every Nigerian knows too well — the frustration of not knowing which car hire company to trust, what a fair price looks like, or who to call when you land at Murtala Muhammed Airport at midnight and need a reliable ride.

We brought together over 50 verified rental companies — from global brands like Hertz and Avis to homegrown Nigerian operators in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Enugu, Kano and beyond — into one easy-to-browse directory, complete with real fleet details and transparent pricing.

How It Works

We Handle the Booking For You

Unlike a basic directory that just lists phone numbers, DriveDir acts as your personal booking agent. You browse, choose, and request — we reach out to the company, confirm availability and pricing, then get back to you. You never need to chase anyone.
